Bridging Global Expertise with Local Solutions – Since 1979

Kawsuf Procurement & Supply Systems Ltd (KPSSL) traces its roots back to 1979, when it began as an engineering and consultancy division before evolving into a fully independent, multidisciplinary consulting firm in 1981. Initially focused on East and Central Africa, we have since expanded our operations across the entire African continent and beyond, serving governments, international donor agencies, private sector operators, and global organizations.

Headquartered in Kampala, KPSSL brings together a diverse network of over 1,000 multicultural professionals—including permanent staff and retained experts from around the world—to deliver locally grounded, globally informed solutions. By blending foreign technological expertise with on-the-ground knowledge, we tackle complex challenges efficiently, minimizing costs without compromising quality. Today, KPSSL stands as a trusted partner for high-impact projects, backed by four decades of excellence and a commitment to bridging global innovation with regional needs.
